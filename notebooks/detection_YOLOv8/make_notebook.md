### **2. Differences Between Experiment Notebooks**

#### **V1: Baseline (detection_YOLOv8_v1.ipynb)**
- **Customization:** None (standard YOLOv8m)
- **Training Config:** LR=0.001, Batch=12, Epochs=300
- **Model Info:** "Standard YOLOv8m (no customization)"

#### **V2: Deeper Backbone (detection_YOLOv8_v2.ipynb)**
- **Customization:** Added 2 Conv layers after backbone layer2
- **Training Config:** LR=0.0005, Batch=12, Epochs=300, cos_lr=True, close_mosaic=10
- **Model Info:** 
  ```
  Customization: Added convolutional layers in backbone
    - Location: After backbone layer2 (C2f module)
    - Added layers: 2 Conv(128, 3x3, stride=1, padding=1)
    - Purpose: Deepen shallow-layer feature extraction
  ```

#### **V3: Shallower Backbone (detection_YOLOv8_v3.ipynb)**
- **Customization:** Reduced C2f repeats in backbone layer4
- **Training Config:** LR=0.001, Batch=20, Epochs=300
- **Model Info:**
  ```
  Customization: Reduced backbone depth by modifying C2f modules
    - Location: Backbone layer4 (P4/16 level)
    - Change: Reduced C2f repeats (lighter model)
    - Benefit: Faster training, reduced overfitting risk
  ```
